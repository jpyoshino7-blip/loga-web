Technical Specifications

High-fidelity capture specifications of Loga.

Still Capture Pipeline

  • ✓

    10-bit YpCbCr Recording

    Captures directly from the native wide-color Apple Log sensor stream utilizing standard AVFoundation pipelines.

  • ✓

    Metal 2 Preview Pipeline

    Applies real-time 3D LUT pixel buffer transformations utilizing optimized CoreImage and Metal rendering destinations.

  • ✓

    Lossless/Lossy Dual Save

    Completely separates the graded RGB JPEG save (saved directly to Camera Roll) and the uncompressed raw 10-bit TIFF archive.
